About the Podcast

Two Chicago friends talking about all their private parts. We met in high school over twenty years ago. We record from a comfortable place. We're like a personal diary entry with other people's opinions and weird perspectives. We answer shameless questions to life's most burning questions with hypothetical questions about our sexy superpowers and Ralph Macchio posters.
